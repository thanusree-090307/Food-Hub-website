Ingredients

1 cup basmati rice (soaked 20–30 min)
500g chicken (with bone preferred)
2 onions (thinly sliced)
2 tomatoes (chopped)
2 green chilies
1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
½ cup curd (yogurt)
3 tbsp oil + 1 tbsp ghee
🌿 Whole Spices
1 bay leaf
1 cinnamon stick
3 cloves
2 cardamom pods
1 star anise (optional)
🌱 Herbs & Extras
Mint leaves
Coriander leaves
12–15 cashews (optional)
½ tsp turmeric
1½ tsp red chili powder
1 tsp garam masala
Salt to taste

Preparation Steps

1️⃣ Marinate Chicken Mix chicken with curd, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, chili powder, salt Marinate for 30 minutes (longer = more flavor)
2️⃣ Cook Base Heat oil + ghee Add whole spices → sauté Add onions → fry till golden Add tomatoes → cook till soft
3️⃣ Cook Chicken Add marinated chicken Cook for 8–10 mins until chicken releases juices
4️⃣ Add Rice Add soaked rice Add mint + coriander Add 2 cups water Adjust salt
5️⃣ Dum Cooking Cover and cook on low flame for 10–15 minutes OR pressure cook (1 whistle) Rest 5–10 minutes → fluff gently
