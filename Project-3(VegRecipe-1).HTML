Ingredients

1 cup rice (basmati or sona masoori)
1 cup thick coconut milk
1 cup water
1 tbsp oil or ghee
1 tsp mustard seeds
1 small onion (sliced)
1-2 green chilies (slit)
1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
Few curry leaves
10-12 cashews
Salt (to taste)
Fresh coriander (for garnish)

Preparation Steps

1️⃣ Cook the Rice Wash rice and soak for 15 minutes Cook with coconut milk + water + salt Keep aside once done (fluffy, not mushy)
2️⃣ Prepare Tempering Heat oil/ghee in a pan Add mustard seeds → let them splutter Add cashews → fry till golden Add curry leaves, green chilies, onions → sauté till soft
3️⃣ Mix Everything Add ginger-garlic paste → cook for 1 minute Add cooked rice → gently mix Adjust salt
4️⃣ Final Touch Garnish with coriander Optional: add a few drops of lemon juice for extra flavor 🍽️ Tips Use thick coconut milk for rich taste Don’t over-stir → rice may break You can add vegetables (carrot, peas) for variation
