Ingredients

1 cup basmati rice
2 cups water
1½ tsp cumin seeds (jeera)
1–2 tbsp ghee (or oil)
1 bay leaf
2 cloves
1 small cinnamon stick
Salt to taste
Fresh coriander (optional)

Preparation Steps

1️⃣ Cook Rice Wash and soak rice for 15–20 minutes Cook with water + salt until fluffy Keep aside (grains should be separate)
2️⃣ Prepare Jeera Tadka Heat ghee in a pan Add cumin seeds → let them crackle Add bay leaf, cloves, cinnamon → sauté
3️⃣ Mix Rice Add cooked rice to the pan Gently mix so grains don’t break
4️⃣ Final Touch Garnish with coriander Optional: add a few drops of lemon juice
Tips
Use ghee for authentic aroma Don’t overcook rice → it becomes sticky You can add fried onions or cashews for extra taste
