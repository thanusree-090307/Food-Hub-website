Ingredients

500g chicken (legs or pieces with bone)
½ cup thick curd (yogurt)
1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
1 tbsp lemon juice
🌶️ Spices
1 tsp red chili powder
½ tsp turmeric
1 tsp garam masala
1 tsp coriander powder
½ tsp cumin powder
Salt to taste
🌿 Extras
1 tbsp oil or melted butter
Few drops red food color (optional for restaurant look)
Chaat masala (for garnish)

Preparation Steps

1️⃣ Marinate Chicken Make deep cuts in chicken Mix curd, spices, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, oil Coat chicken well Marinate for at least 2–4 hours (overnight = best)
2️⃣ Cooking Methods
🔥 Oven Method
Preheat oven to 200°C Bake for 25–30 mins (flip halfway) Brush with butter for smoky finish
🍳 Pan Method
Cook on medium flame with little oil Cover and cook → then roast till slightly charred
🔥 Grill Method (Best)
Grill on high heat until nicely charred
