Ingredients

500g mutton (with bone)
2 tbsp oil
1 onion (finely chopped)
2 tomatoes (chopped)
1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
2 green chilies
🌶️ Spices
½ tsp turmeric
1½ tsp red chili powder
1 tsp coriander powder
½ tsp cumin powder
1 tsp garam masala
Salt to taste
🌿 Extras
Curry leaves
Coriander leaves
1½ cups water

Preparation Steps

1️⃣ Cook Base Heat oil → add curry leaves Add onions → sauté till golden Add ginger-garlic paste → cook
2️⃣ Prepare Masala Add tomatoes → cook till soft Add turmeric, chili powder, coriander, cumin, salt Cook till oil separates
3️⃣ Cook Mutton Add mutton → mix well Cook for 8–10 minutes
4️⃣ Pressure Cook Add water Pressure cook for 4–5 whistles (until soft)
5️⃣ Final Touch Open lid → simmer if needed for thick gravy Add garam masala Garnish with coriander

Tips for Perfect Mutton Curry
Cook mutton properly → should be soft & juicy Use bone-in pieces for more flavor Slow cooking improves taste Add coconut paste or poppy seed paste for richer gravy (optional)
