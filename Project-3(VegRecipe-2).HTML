Ingredients

1 cup basmati rice (soaked 20 min)
1 cup thick coconut milk
1 cup water
1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, potato)
1 onion (sliced)
2 green chilies (slit)
1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
2 tbsp oil + 1 tbsp ghee
🌿 Whole Spices
1 bay leaf
1-inch cinnamon stick
2 cloves
2 cardamom pods
1 star anise (optional)
🌱 Herbs & Extras
Few mint leaves
Few coriander leaves
10–12 cashews
Salt to taste

Preparation Steps

1️⃣ Fry Base Heat oil + ghee Add whole spices → fry till aromatic Add cashews → fry golden Add onions → sauté till light brown
2️⃣ Add Flavors Add green chilies + ginger-garlic paste Add vegetables → sauté 3–4 mins Add mint & coriander leaves/
3️⃣ Cook Biryani Add soaked rice Pour coconut milk + water Add salt → mix gently
4️⃣ Final Cooking Cover and cook on low flame OR pressure cook (1 whistle) Let it rest 5 minutes Fluff gently
Tips for Perfect Biryani
Use basmati rice for long grains Don’t add too much coconut milk → can become sticky Cook on low flame (dum style) for best flavor Add a squeeze of lemon at end for balance
