Ingredients

1 cup basmati rice (soaked 30 min)
500g mutton (with bone)
2 onions (thinly sliced)
2 tomatoes (chopped)
2 green chilies
1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
½ cup curd (yogurt)
3 tbsp oil + 1 tbsp ghee
🌿 Whole Spices
1 bay leaf
1 cinnamon stick
3 cloves
2 cardamom pods
1 star anise
🌱 Herbs & Extras
Mint leaves
Coriander leaves
12–15 cashews
½ tsp turmeric
1½ tsp red chili powder
1 tsp garam masala
Salt to taste

Preparation Steps

1️⃣ Marinate Mutton Mix mutton with curd, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, chili powder, salt Marinate for at least 30–60 minutes (more = better taste)
2️⃣ Cook Mutton Heat oil + ghee Add whole spices → sauté Add onions → fry till golden Add tomatoes → cook till soft Add marinated mutton → cook 10 mins Add 1 cup water → pressure cook (3–4 whistles)
3️⃣ Add Rice Add soaked rice Add mint + coriander Adjust salt
4️⃣ Dum Cooking Cover tightly and cook on low flame 10–15 minutes Let it rest 10 minutes
