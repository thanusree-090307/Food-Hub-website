Ingredients

4–5 small brinjals (eggplant), slit
2 tbsp oil
1 onion (finely chopped)
2 tomatoes (chopped)
1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
2 green chilies
🌶️ Spices
½ tsp turmeric
1 tsp red chili powder
1 tsp coriander powder
½ tsp garam masala
Salt to taste
🥜 Masala Paste
2 tbsp peanuts
1 tbsp sesame seeds
2 tbsp grated coconut (fresh or dry)
🌿 Extras
Curry leaves
Coriander leaves

Preparation Steps

1️⃣ Roast & Grind Dry roast peanuts + sesame + coconut Cool and grind into smooth paste
2️⃣ Fry Brinjals Heat oil → shallow fry brinjals until slightly soft Keep aside
3️⃣ Prepare Masala In same pan, add oil Add curry leaves + onions → sauté till golden Add ginger-garlic paste → cook Add tomatoes → cook till soft
4️⃣ Add Spices & Paste Add turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, salt Add prepared masala paste → mix well
5️⃣ Cook Curry Add fried brinjals Add little water → simmer 10 minutes Add garam masala
6️⃣ Final Touch Garnish with coriander leaves
