Ingredients

1 cup basmati rice (soaked 20 min)
200g mushrooms (cleaned & sliced)
1 large onion (sliced)
2 tomatoes (chopped)
2 green chilies
1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
½ cup curd (yogurt)
2 tbsp oil + 1 tbsp ghee
🌿 Whole Spices
1 bay leaf
1 cinnamon stick
2 cloves
2 cardamom pods
1 star anise (optional)
🌱 Herbs & Extras
Mint leaves
Coriander leaves
10–12 cashews
½ tsp turmeric
1 tsp red chili powder
1 tsp garam masala
Salt to taste

Preparation Steps

1️⃣ Fry Mushrooms Heat a little oil Sauté mushrooms on high flame until water evaporates Keep aside
2️⃣ Prepare Masala Heat oil + ghee Add whole spices → sauté Add cashews → fry golden Add onions → cook till brown Add ginger-garlic paste
3️⃣ Build Flavor Add tomatoes → cook till soft Add turmeric, chili powder, salt Add curd → mix well Add mushrooms + mint + coriander
4️⃣ Cook Biryani Add soaked rice Add 2 cups water Mix gently
5️⃣ Final Cooking Cook on low flame (dum) OR pressure cook (1 whistle) Rest 5 minutes → fluff
