Ingredients

1 cup basmati rice (soaked 20 min)
4 boiled eggs
2 onions (sliced)
2 tomatoes (chopped)
2 green chilies
1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
½ cup curd (yogurt)
2 tbsp oil + 1 tbsp ghee
🌿 Whole Spices
1 bay leaf
1 cinnamon stick
2 cloves
2 cardamom pods
🌱 Extras
Mint leaves
Coriander leaves
½ tsp turmeric
1 tsp red chili powder
1 tsp garam masala
Salt to taste

Preparation Steps

1️⃣ Prepare Eggs Boil eggs → peel Lightly fry eggs with a pinch of salt & turmeric (optional for extra taste)
2️⃣ Cook Base Heat oil + ghee Add whole spices → sauté Add onions → fry till golden Add ginger-garlic paste
3️⃣ Make Masala Add tomatoes → cook till soft Add turmeric, chili powder, salt Add curd → mix well
4️⃣ Add Rice & Eggs Add soaked rice Add 2 cups water Place eggs on top Add mint & coriander
5️⃣ Dum Cooking Cover and cook on low flame 10–15 mins OR pressure cook (1 whistle) Rest 5 minutes → fluff gently
