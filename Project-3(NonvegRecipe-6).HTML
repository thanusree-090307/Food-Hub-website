Ingredients

4–5 fish pieces (any firm fish like rohu, tilapia, or king fish)
2 tbsp oil (for frying)
1 tbsp lemon juice
🌶️ Masala
1 tsp red chili powder
½ tsp turmeric
1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
1 tsp coriander powder
½ tsp cumin powder
Salt to taste
🌿 Extras
Curry leaves
Rice flour or semolina (optional, for extra crispiness)

Preparation Steps

1️⃣ Marinate Fish Clean fish and pat dry Mix all masala + lemon juice Coat fish evenly Marinate for 20–30 minutes
2️⃣ Fry Fish Heat oil in a pan Add curry leaves Place fish gently Fry on medium flame Cook both sides until golden & crispy
3️⃣ Final Touch Remove and rest on tissue Sprinkle chaat masala or lemon juice (optional)

Tips for Perfect Fish Fry
Use fresh fish for best taste Don’t flip too often → fish may break Add rice flour for crispy coating Cook on medium flame → ensures inside cooks well
