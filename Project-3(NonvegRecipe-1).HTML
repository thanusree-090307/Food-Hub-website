Ingredients

500g chicken (with bone preferred)
2 tbsp oil
1 onion (finely chopped)
2 tomatoes (chopped)
1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
2 green chilies
🌶️ Spices
½ tsp turmeric
1 tsp red chili powder
1 tsp coriander powder
½ tsp garam masala
Salt to taste
🌿 Extras
Curry leaves
Coriander leaves
½ cup water

Preparation Steps

1️⃣ Fry Base Heat oil → add curry leaves Add onions → sauté till golden Add ginger-garlic paste → cook
2️⃣ Build Masala Add tomatoes → cook till soft Add turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, salt Cook till oil separates
3️⃣ Cook Chicken Add chicken pieces → mix well Cook for 5–7 mins (chicken releases juices)
4️⃣ Add Water & Simmer Add water → cover and cook 15–20 mins Stir occasionally
5️⃣ Final Touch Add garam masala Garnish with coriander
Tips
Use bone-in chicken for more flavor Cook on medium flame for best taste Add a little coconut milk for richer curry (optional) Rest for 10 minutes before serving → enhances flavor
