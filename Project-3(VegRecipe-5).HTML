Ingredients

200g Paneer (cubed)
2 tbsp butter
1 tbsp oil
2 tomatoes (pureed)
1 onion (roughly chopped)
1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
10–12 cashews
½ cup fresh cream
1 tsp red chili powder
½ tsp turmeric
1 tsp garam masala
1 tsp sugar
Salt to taste

Preparation Steps

1️⃣ Make Base Paste Heat 1 tsp oil Add onion + cashews → sauté Add tomato puree → cook till soft Cool and blend into smooth paste
2️⃣ Cook Gravy Heat butter + oil Add ginger-garlic paste → sauté Add prepared paste → cook till oil separates
3️⃣ Add Spices Add chili powder, turmeric, salt, sugar Mix well
4️⃣ Add Paneer Add paneer cubes Add a little water if needed Simmer for 5 minutes
5️⃣ Final Touch Add cream + garam masala Mix gently and cook 2 more minutes
