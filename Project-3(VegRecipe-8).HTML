Ingredients

🥕 For Manchurian Balls
1 cup cabbage (finely chopped)
½ cup carrot (grated)
½ cup capsicum (finely chopped)
2 tbsp cornflour
2 tbsp maida
1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
Salt & pepper to taste
Oil (for frying)
🍜 For Sauce
1 tbsp oil
1 tbsp garlic (finely chopped)
1 tbsp ginger (chopped)
2 tbsp spring onions
1 tbsp soy sauce
1 tbsp tomato ketchup
1 tsp chili sauce
1 tsp vinegar
1 tsp cornflour (mixed in water)
Salt & pepper

Preparation Steps

1️⃣ Make Balls Mix all vegetables + cornflour + maida + spices Form small balls Deep fry till golden and crispy
2️⃣ Prepare Sauce Heat oil → add garlic & ginger Add spring onions → sauté Add soy sauce, ketchup, chili sauce, vinegar
3️⃣ Make Gravy Add little water Add cornflour slurry → cook till thick
4️⃣ Combine Add fried balls → mix gently Cook 2 minutes
