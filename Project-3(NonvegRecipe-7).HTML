Ingredients

250g prawns (cleaned & deveined)
2 tbsp oil
1 tbsp lemon juice
🌶️ Masala
1 tsp red chili powder
½ tsp turmeric
1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
1 tsp coriander powder
½ tsp cumin powder
Salt to taste
🌿 Extras
Curry leaves
1 onion (optional, sliced)
Rice flour (optional, for crispiness)

Preparation Steps

1️⃣ Marinate Prawns Mix prawns with masala + lemon juice Marinate for 15–20 minutes
2️⃣ Fry Prawns Heat oil in a pan Add curry leaves (and onions if using) Add prawns → cook on medium-high flame Fry for 5–7 minutes until cooked and slightly crispy
3️⃣ Final Touch Sprinkle lemon juice or coriander Optional: add a pinch of pepper for extra spice

Tips for Perfect Prawns Fry
Don’t overcook → prawns become rubbery Cook on high flame for best texture Add little rice flour for crispy coating Fresh prawns = best taste
